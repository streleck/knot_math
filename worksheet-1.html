Worksheet 1

Drawing & Naming

Below are some knot names and cells for drawing. Feel free to use paper and pencil or even real rope instead. Paper and pen is my preferred method. Try to make the knots look good (by whatever criteria you like). This is important because to really 'get' knots, you must appreciate the beauty in them! You can reveal the answers by clicking the 'check answer' button. But if you don't try first, you will be cheating and will feel shame even though no one else will know.
